What comes up if your hear: Logistic? May you think of two positions A and B, where someone deliver something from A to B. Roughly speaking, that’s it. To be more accurate imagine the following: You got a Logistic company, a costumer wants an offer to ship his items from his store (A) his goal(B). This is a typically task from a logistic company, finding out the shortest and cheapest way. For this purpose I developed this tool. You, as a logistic company contact the trucker which is near to the cheapest container or more near to the starting point. That would be a smart way, to get a short distance between any points he has to drive -please keep in mind that most time you have to pay for the Trucker oil as well. But what is if the container which is nearer to the Trucker offers a higher price as the container lessor 50 km more away. Therefor I developed this tool, which calculates the whole Amount of prices for each location and dependency.
