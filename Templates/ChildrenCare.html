The most urgent thing for Germany. A new digitalization strategy, to optimize our education cornerstones. Children Check is a workflow for kindergarten employees designed for state processes: -Upload Children data like name and lastname in to the database -Check all children inside a group -trigger the workflow to send the mail to local community/health authority You can also: -restrict employees permissions -use it via mobile(mobilefriendly-comming soon) -Edit children data Children Check is a django Application based on simple Web tools.
