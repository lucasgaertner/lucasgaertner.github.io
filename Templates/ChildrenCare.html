The most urgent thing for Germany. A new digitalization strategy, to optimize our education cornerstones. During the last year Germany accepted a big deficit in our digitalization strategy. In the past we turned very much towards the USA. Which processes have established themselves and which open up new markets. In this context, we have always copied from our neighbors. The courage to digitize must change so that we can continue to be strong as a nation. I guess sometimes we have to fall, before we have a good standing.

This workflow covers a simple step in kindergartens. Every day employees have to submit a list to the health department of which children are in kindergarten today. At the moment it is still running physically, an attendance list is filled out and entered by the supervisor in the computer and sent by email. This process is outdated and out of date. Digitization should save us the time that we would otherwise use for routine processes. When simple workflows save 5-10 minutes per day at each point of contact, we gain more time for important work. The work that can only be done by humans.

Here is a technical overview:
Children Check is a workflow for kindergarten employees designed for state processes: -Upload Children data like name and lastname in to the database -Check all children inside a group -trigger the workflow to send the mail to local community/health authority You can also: -restrict employees permissions -use it via mobile(mobilefriendly-comming soon) -Edit children data Children Check is a django Application based on simple Web tools.
